About Persipica

Specialized consultancy for Generative Engine Optimization.

2 min read · Updated April 2026

Who is Persipica?

Persipica is a Generative Engine Optimisation consultancy helping companies get cited and recommended by AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

The organisations we work with are competing in crowded categories where being recommended by AI at the right moment directly impacts pipeline.

Founder

Ilkan Ozemre

Ilkan founded Persipica after noticing a pattern in go-to-market work with B2B technology companies: pipeline was increasingly coming from AI-generated shortlists, but companies had no visibility into whether they appeared in those outputs.

He spent 18 months building a methodology to measure and diagnose AI citation gaps before founding Persipica. Every engagement is run by Ilkan directly.

Why Diagnosis Before Tooling

  • Monitoring without diagnosis is noise. AI visibility dashboards show you citation rate trends. They don't tell you why discovery queries return 0% or which content changes would move the needle.
  • Every company starts at 0% discovery citation. It is not because AI ignores them. Their content is not structured for machine extraction. This is a solvable, technical problem.
  • The window for early-mover advantage is open now. AI-driven referral traffic grew 975% year-over-year in 2025. The companies that close citation gaps first will be hardest to displace.
