## What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the discipline of ensuring your company appears in AI-generated recommendations when buyers ask questions relevant to your category. Just as SEO shaped how brands appeared in Google results, GEO shapes how brands appear in AI-generated answers.

Most companies score well when AI is asked about them by name, but score 0% when a buyer asks AI to recommend a solution without naming anyone. GEO closes that gap.

## Key findings from our research

- **0% citation rate** at discovery and buying intent stages across every company audited
- **100% citation rate** when companies were asked about by name
- **14.2% conversion rate** from AI search traffic vs 2.8% from Google organic (5x differential)
- **975% year-over-year growth** in AI-driven referral traffic
