Privacy Policy

How we handle data for audits and consultancy.

2 min read · Updated April 2026

Data Collection

We collect minimal data required to provide our services. This includes contact information provided via our contact forms and technical data required to run AI visibility audits for your brand.

We do not sell your data to third parties. Data collected during audits is used strictly for the purpose of providing you with findings and recommendations.

Audit Data

The query sets and AI responses generated during a Persipica audit are stored securely. We may use anonymized, aggregated data from multiple audits to publish research findings (e.g., industry-wide citation averages), but individual brand data is never disclosed without explicit permission.
