Persipica vs Profound

Profound now positions itself as a broader AI marketing platform: answer engine visibility, prompt volume research, agent analytics, shopping visibility, and autonomous agents in one system. Persipica Pulse is narrower by design. It is built for teams that need prompt-level evidence, source-level attribution, and a clearer read on which visibility changes matter commercially.

A buyer should be able to choose either tool for a clear reason.

Profound fits teams that want one platform for AI visibility, prompt demand research, crawler analytics, shopping visibility, and agent workflows.

Persipica fits teams that need to prove which source moved an answer, keep the underlying prompt-and-response evidence, and connect that change to commercial exposure.

  • Profound may fit better for Fortune 500 brand, ecommerce, or analytics teams centralizing several AI marketing workflows.
  • Persipica may fit better for B2B SaaS and professional services teams that need defensible evidence in a smaller operating surface.

Where Profound broadens, Persipica deepens.

While platforms like Profound focus on workflow breadth, Persipica is built for evidentiary depth. We provide the underlying proof that makes AI monitoring defensible.

Pulse estimates the pipeline exposure behind a visibility drop. We call that Revenue at Risk.
Pulse separates cited sources into owned, earned, community, and review categories. We call that Attribution Breakdown.
Pulse re-scores the same answer with competitors swapped in so you can see where you lose head to head. We call that Virtual Competitor Re-scoring.

Two different answers to the same question.

Profound and Persipica are optimized for different kinds of teams and different levels of operating complexity.

Choose Profound if...

Your organization needs an all-in-one AI marketing suite that handles everything from demand research and crawler analytics to autonomous shopping workflows.

  • You are a large brand or ecommerce team owning multiple AI workflows.
  • You need to track prompt volume demand and agent behavior across surfaces.
  • Consolidating AI marketing jobs into one platform is your top priority.

Choose Persipica if...

Your team needs to show exactly which citation source moved an answer, keep the evidence on file, and connect that shift to commercial pipeline risk.

  • You need auditable citation evidence to explain recommendation shifts.
  • You need to model the revenue impact of visibility losses for leadership.
  • You prefer a focused monitoring layer that is easy to defend internally.
