# What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the discipline of ensuring that a company, product, or expertise is cited and recommended by large language model (LLM) systems, such as ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity, when users ask questions relevant to that company's category.

## How GEO differs from SEO

SEO optimizes for search engine ranking: getting a page to appear high in a list of links that a user can choose from. GEO optimizes for AI citation: getting a company mentioned, described, or recommended within a synthesized AI-generated answer.

The key differences:

| Dimension | SEO | GEO |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| **Output** | A ranked list of links | A synthesized answer that may cite zero or multiple sources |
| **User action** | User clicks a link and evaluates the page | User reads the answer directly, no click required |
| **Ranking signal** | Backlinks, domain authority, keyword relevance | Training data representation, semantic density, third-party corroboration |
| **Content goal** | Rank for a keyword | Be cited as the answer to a question |
| **Measurement** | Page rank, organic traffic, CTR | Citation rate, share of voice, sentiment accuracy |

## Why GEO matters for enterprise companies

AI platforms are becoming the primary research tool for B2B buyers. When a buyer asks ChatGPT "what are the best tools for [problem]?" and your company does not appear, you lose pipeline you will never know existed.

Key data points:

- **14.2%**: AI search visitors convert at 14.2% compared to 2.8% from Google organic (Source: Opollo, 2026 AI Search Benchmark Report)
- **975%**: Year-over-year growth in AI-driven referral traffic (Source: Opollo, 2026)
- **73%**: Percentage of B2B buyers who use AI tools in their research process (Source: Loganix, 2026 AI Buying Behavior Analysis)
- **0%**: Citation rate at discovery and buying intent stages for every company Persipica has audited (Source: Persipica first-party research, April 2026)

## The two types of AI visibility

### 1. Brand visibility
When someone asks AI about your company by name ("tell me about [Company]"), you almost always appear. This is not the problem.

### 2. Discovery visibility
When someone asks AI to recommend a solution without naming anyone ("what tools can help me [solve problem]?"), most companies do not appear. This is the problem GEO solves.
